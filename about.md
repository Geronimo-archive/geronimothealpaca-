# About this archive

This archive has been created to preserve and make publicly accessible original documents relating to Geronimo the alpaca.

## Document handling
- Documents are published in their original form wherever possible.
- Files are not altered, edited, or annotated.
- Where redactions exist in the original document, they remain unchanged.

## Summaries
- Short summaries are provided to describe the nature and context of each document.
- Summaries are factual and descriptive and do not seek to interpret conclusions.

## Publication approach
- Documents are added chronologically as they become available.
- Updates or additional material are appended rather than replacing earlier records.
- If an error is identified, corrections will be clearly noted rather than removed.

This archive is maintained in the public interest to support transparency, accountability, and independent examination of the published material.
